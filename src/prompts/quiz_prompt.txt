====================================================================================

Your goal is to test COMPREHENSION, CHARACTER UNDERSTANDING, THEMES, and INFERENCE.

IF SUBJECT IS SCIENCE/MATHEMATICS/PROCEDURAL (Concepts, Processes, Systems):
=============================================================================

Your goal is to test CONCEPT UNDERSTANDING, PROCESS KNOWLEDGE, and PROBLEM APPLICATION.

QUESTION DISTRIBUTION (aim for this across 10 questions):
- 2 questions on BASIC RECALL - What is X? Define Y? (tests factual knowledge)
- 3 questions on CONCEPT UNDERSTANDING - How does X work? Why is X important? What is the relationship between X and Y? (tests comprehension)
- 2 questions on PROCESS/MECHANISM - Explain the steps of X? What happens when X occurs? (tests procedural knowledge)
- 2 questions on APPLICATION & ANALYSIS - Apply concept X to scenario Y? Why would X lead to Y? (tests application and problem-solving)
- 1 question on COMPARISON/CONTRAST - How is X different from Y? What is similar between X and Y? (tests analytical thinking)

EXAMPLE SCIENCE QUESTIONS:
- "Which of the following is a primary function of [organ/system] in the body?"
- "Explain why [process] occurs in living organisms?"
- "If [scenario], what would happen to [outcome]?"
- "What is the relationship between [concept A] and [concept B]?"
- "Which statement correctly describes how [process/structure] works?"

IMPORTANT GUIDELINES FOR MATHEMATICS QUESTIONS:

- Questions may include **numbers, ratios, or simple expressions** when needed.
- Focus on **understanding of method**, not heavy calculations.
- Avoid very long calculations or multi-step numerical solving.
- Ensure numbers are **small and appropriate for grade {class_grade}.**
- When using numbers, ensure only **one option gives the correct result.**
- Mathematical expressions should be **clear and readable in plain text.**

EXAMPLE MATHEMATICS QUESTIONS:

- "Which of the following ratios is equivalent to 2:3?"
- "If two quantities increase in the same ratio, what type of relationship is this?"
- "Which method can be used to divide a number in a given ratio?"
- "Which situation represents a proportional relationship?"
- "What happens to one quantity if the other increases in an inverse proportion?"
