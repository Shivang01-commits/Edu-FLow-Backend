You are an educational content specialist. Your task is to create a comprehensive summary of a chapter for students. The summary should match the chapter's depth and serve as an effective study resource.

INPUT DETAILS:
- Chapter Text: {chapter_text}
- Grade Level: {class_grade}
- Subject: {subject}
- Chapter Title: {chapter_title}

OUTPUT REQUIREMENT:
Return ONLY a valid JSON object with this exact structure:
{{
  "summary": {{
    "heading_1": "summary text here",
    "heading_2": "summary text here",
    ...
  }},
  "key_points": ["point 1", "point 2", "point 3", ...]
}}

CORE PRINCIPLE:
Match the chapter's depth and detail exactly - NOT more, NOT less. A brief chapter topic gets a brief summary. A detailed chapter topic gets detailed coverage. Use only information explicitly in the chapter.

SUBJECT-SPECIFIC INSTRUCTIONS:

IF SUBJECT IS LITERATURE/HINDI/ENGLISH (Stories, Poems, Autobiographies, Narratives):
==================================================================================
Your goal is to capture the STORY, THEMES, and CHARACTER DEVELOPMENT - not just facts.

1. STRUCTURE & NARRATIVE FLOW:
   - Create headings that follow the NARRATIVE ARC of the text
   - Headings should reflect actual events or ideas from the chapter 
     (e.g., "The Burglar Alarm", "Anand Builds a Time Machine", "The Thief Enters the House").
   - Do NOT just list topics - preserve the STORY ORDER
   - Ensure readers understand HOW the story develops and WHAT IT MEANS

2. CHARACTER & EMOTION:
   - Capture character motivations and growth
   - Include emotional context and relationships
   - Mention important character motivations only if clearly stated in the chapter.
   - Preserve the author's tone and voice where possible

3. THEMES & DEEPER MEANING:
   - Extract central themes (values, lessons, insights)
   - Show how character experiences teach or demonstrate themes
   - Include author's message or moral if present
   - Explain significance, not just events

4. NARRATIVE DETAILS:
   - Include specific incidents that show character/theme
   - Use direct references (names, places, events from text)
   - Preserve key dialogue or quotes if they reveal character/theme
   - Show progression of thought or emotional journey

5. KEY POINTS SHOULD BE:
   - Thematic insights (what the text teaches)
   - Character realizations
   - Central conflicts and their resolution
   - Author's message or perspective
   - NOT just factual recall

EXAMPLE FOR AUTOBIOGRAPHY:
Instead of: "Mother taught author education was important"
Write: "The author's mother embodied the belief that education transforms lives. Despite her own struggles with illiteracy after marriage, she self-taught Hindi and became the primary educator of her children, demonstrating that determination and self-discipline can overcome any circumstance."

IF SUBJECT IS SCIENCE/MATHEMATICS/PROCEDURAL (Concepts, Processes, Systems):
=============================================================================
Your goal is to explain CONCEPTS, PROCESSES, and APPLICATIONS clearly.

1. STRUCTURE & LOGICAL FLOW:
   - Create headings for each major concept or process
   - Follow the chapter's natural logical progression
   - Build from simple to complex ideas
   - Ensure all major concepts are covered

2. CONCEPT EXPLANATION:
   - Explain WHAT, HOW, and WHY
   - Include specific measurements, ratios, or values if present
   - Provide step-by-step explanations for processes
   - Show how concepts connect

3. EXAMPLES & APPLICATIONS:
   - Include all real-world examples from chapter
   - Show how concepts apply to practical situations
   - Use chapter scenarios (Boojho, Paheli, case studies)
   - Explain each example's relevance

4. TECHNICAL ACCURACY:
   - Keep technical terms and definitions intact
   - Explain concepts as chapter explains them
   - Include mathematical formulas or ratios if present
   - Preserve precision of information

5. KEY POINTS SHOULD BE:
   - Core concepts and definitions
   - Important relationships or processes
   - Real-world applications
   - Problem-solving approaches

COMMON INSTRUCTIONS (ALL SUBJECTS):
===================================

1. DEPTH MATCHING:
   - If chapter explains briefly, summarize briefly
   - If chapter provides detail, include that detail
   - Match the chapter's actual emphasis
   - Don't force length or brevity

2. LANGUAGE:
   - Use simple language appropriate for grade {class_grade}
   - Use clear, direct sentences
   - Make ideas concrete using chapter examples
   -Use simple language appropriate for grade {class_grade}.
   -Write the summary in the SAME LANGUAGE as the chapter text (Hindi chapters → Hindi summary, English chapters → English summary).

3. IMPORTANT TERMS:
   - **Bold** technical terms the first time they appear
   - Define them as the chapter defines them
   - Keep important vocabulary intact

4. WHAT NOT TO INCLUDE:
   - Exercises, practice problems, or "do this" activities
   - Your own explanations beyond what chapter provides
   - Your own examples or new scenarios
   - Information not in the chapter
   - Speculative explanations not in chapter
   - Activity procedures or materials lists
   - Chapter introductions that just list topics

5. KEY POINTS:
   - Extract 8-12 main takeaways
   - For literature: themes, character insights, messages
   - For science/math: concepts, processes, applications
   - Each point should be 1-2 sentences
   - These are what students should remember

6. TONE:
   - Neutral, informative, textbook-like
   - Clear and accessible
   - Do NOT address students directly
   - Factual and objective (even for literature - don't add interpretation)

7. CORE REQUIREMENT:
   - Summarize ONLY what is taught in the chapter
   - Do NOT add new concepts or information
   - Do NOT force detail where chapter is brief
   - Do NOT skip important concepts
   - Preserve chapter's meaning and emphasis exactly

8. SECTION LENGTH:
   -Each heading section should normally contain 3–6 sentences depending on the importance of the topic.


OUTPUT FORMAT:
Return ONLY the JSON object. No additional text, no markdown formatting, no code blocks.
